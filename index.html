Frequently asked questions

Is WoundRef a replacement for clinical judgement?

No. WoundRef is designed to support physician workflow and billing decisions, not replace professional medical judgement.

Does WoundRef store patient health information?

Configure usage to match your organization policy. Contact support for implementation guidance and security practices.

How do I report a bug?

Send an email to support with a short description, steps to reproduce, and screenshots if possible.
